NEW YORK

New York is situated across a region of contrast—from the Atlantic shores of Long Island and the skyscrapers of Manhattan through the rivers, mountains, and lakes of upstate New York to the plains of the Great Lakes region. With canals, railroads, and highways, New York is a principal gateway to the west from the Middle Atlantic and New England states and a hub for travel to and from much of the country. The cities of the state—from New York City through Albany, Utica, and Syracuse to Rochester and Buffalo on the Great Lakes—and their suburbs are home to more than four-fifths of all New Yorkers.Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building and sprawling Central Park. Broadway theater is staged in neon-lit Times Square.

Expenses break down

⦿ Airfare: 900$ per person so 1800 for two of you. (Round trip from Indian metropolitan cities)

⦿ Accommodation: Opt for cheap Airbnb and Hostels spending not more than 60$ a night.

⦿ Food and Drinks: Since you are visiting one of the finest culinary destinations in the world, I highly recommend to splurge a bit here. I would spend at least 100$ a day and that's really not much in NYC.

⦿ Attractions and Daily Commute: 60$ a day.

Total Expenses:

Approximately should be 4000$ USD ~ 2.61 lakhs INR.

Sources: Experienced Travellers.
Must-do and Must-see

⦿ Visit the MoMA, The MET and The Tenement Museum.

⦿ Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge.

⦿ Visit the Brooklyn Heights Promenade for spectacular views of Manhattan.

⦿ Visit the new One World Tower

⦿ Take a stroll or bike in the Central Park.

⦿ Walk around Washington Square Park.

⦿ Check out Times Square (if that's your thing).

⦿ Explore the historic and eclectic Lower East side. It has some of the best graffitis in the world.

⦿ Watch a Broadway show and an Improv Show at UCB.

⦿ Explore the historic and cultural Harlem.

⦿ Enjoy the serene walk along the Brownstones of West Village (You may just run into Sarah Jessica Parker).

⦿ Watch a Rangers game at the MSG (if you arrive in early April)

⦿ Bike along the Hudson on the West side.

⦿ Visit the Cloisters in Upper Manhattan

Must-eats and Must-drink

⦿ Eat Pizza at Lombardi's, Grimaldi's (right below Brooklyn bridge) or Difara.

⦿ Get the Smoked Salmon or Everything Bagel at Russ and Daughter's Cafe in Lower East Side.

⦿ Grab a Chocolate Chip Walnut cookie at Levaine Bakery in Upper West Side.

⦿ Stand in line (for up to 2 hours) to get your hands on Cronuts at the famous Dominique Ansel Bakery in Soho.

⦿ If you are into ethnic food, there is no better city in the world than NYC - venture out to Flushing, Queens for Chinese or Indian food.

⦿ Slurp Ramen at Ippudo or Totto Ramen. Cherish Greek food in Astoria, Queens.

⦿ Sip cocktails at one of many speakeasies in the City - Angel's Share, Employees Only, PDT, etc.
